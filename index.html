About Pauliset Agro Company

Your satisfaction is our target

Pauliset & Son's Global Ltd., take pride in being an outstanding agro company specialize in the cultivation, production, buying, and selling of high-quality cocoa bean and palm oil products. Established in 2012 by our visionary C.E.O Mr. Sunday Etta; a License Buying Agent(LBA), our company has become a symbol of excellence and examplary in the agricultural sector in Nigeria and Africa as a whole.
